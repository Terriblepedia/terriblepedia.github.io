English

English is one of the most widely spoken languages in the world, with over 1.5 billion speakers across different continents. What makes it unique is not just the number of people who use it, but also its incredible adaptability.

A Language of Borrowed Words

Unlike many languages that remain relatively consistent, English thrives on absorbing words from others. From French we borrowed restaurant and ballet, from German came kindergarten, and from Hindi we use shampoo and bungalow. This patchwork of influences makes English both rich and sometimes confusing.

Global Communication Tool

English serves as the “lingua franca” of the modern age. Whether in business meetings, academic journals, or online platforms, English is often the default medium for communication. This role is reinforced by its presence in science, technology, and entertainment.
